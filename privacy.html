ZordPath Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

ZordPath is a children's educational game. We take privacy seriously, especially for young users.

Data Collection

ZordPath does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data (save files, progress, scores) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to any server.

No Accounts Required

ZordPath does not require user accounts, login, email addresses, or any personal information to play.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, cookies, or advertising networks.

No In-App Purchases

ZordPath contains no in-app purchases or microtransactions.

No Advertising

ZordPath contains no advertisements of any kind.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

ZordPath is designed for children and fully complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 or any other age group.

Data Storage

Game progress is saved locally on your device. Clearing your browser data or deleting the app will remove all saved progress. We have no ability to access, recover, or view your game data.

Third-Party Services

ZordPath uses Google Fonts (Press Start 2P) for display purposes. No other third-party services are used.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the ZordPath team via zordpath.com.
