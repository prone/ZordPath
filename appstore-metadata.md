## Description
Embark on an epic adventure through Logic Land! ZordPath is a children's educational RPG that teaches real logic and critical thinking through exploration, battles, and quizzes.

EXPLORE MAGICAL WORLDS
Journey through caves, forests, mountains, beaches, temples, and a mystery island. Each area is filled with unique creatures called Zords to discover and collect.

LEARN REAL LOGIC
15 interactive lessons covering everything from basic true/false statements to advanced topics like set theory, causal reasoning, and paradoxes. Based on university-level logic curriculum, adapted for kids.

CATCH AND BATTLE ZORDS
Catch wild Zords using ZordCages, build your team, and battle trainers in Pokemon-style turn-based combat. Answer quiz questions correctly to power up your attacks and catch rare creatures.

CHALLENGE YOURSELF
- Challenge Mode: Test your knowledge in an endless quiz streak with multiplier scoring
- Logic Runes: Collect 20 ancient symbols of logic hidden in houses across the world
- Report Card: Track your progress with detailed strengths and weaknesses analysis

FEATURES
- 15 logic lessons with 210+ questions
- 6 interactive quiz types (truth tables, pattern matching, logic circuits, and more)
- 50+ unique pixel-art Zords to catch
- Pokemon-style Zord battles with elemental advantages
- Explorable houses with hidden collectibles
- Save system with 3 slots
- Animated ferry rides between islands
- Downloadable PDF report cards
- Full touch controls for iPad
- No ads, no in-app purchases

Perfect for ages 6-14. Makes logic fun!
